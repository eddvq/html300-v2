About this project

This project was is a work in progress for the final deliverable for the UW Front End Certification program. The reason I chose plants is because as a Seattle transplant, I don't know much of the native plant life and wanted to learn more. My idea is through the use of APIs be able to build a website that helps tell you the types of plants that could be around you.

This project was built using HTML, Javascript, and CSS. The APIs that were used are TBD.

Featured Plants

Rhododendron

The rhododendron is a gorgeous flower that blooms typically blooms in the spring but has also been seen blooming in other seasons. It is a flower that can be found in many yards and is usually used as a hedge plant. Be careful! Some species of this flower have been reported to be toxic to animals. Look but don't eat!

Water Lily

Who knew a popular rest stop for frogs also had a beautiful blossom? The water lily is known to live in still shallow waters and will be found in the edges of ponds and lakes.They bloom from May to september and the bloom lasts around four days.
